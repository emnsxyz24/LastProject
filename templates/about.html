About us

Established in May 2020, by 2 young people who started from a small building in an area in Pangkalanbun, Central Kalimantan. Imaji comes to be enjoyed by all circles, from coffee lovers to those who are not. Providing the best service is the main characteristic of imaji, so that customers get the best products and vibes. For the theme of Imaji itself is minimalist like coming home.

Let's work together!

let's mix caffeine and collaboration, stir up some magic, and make waves in this caffeine-fueled journey of ours. Together, let's turn coffee into creativity, one sip at a time.
